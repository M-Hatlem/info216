This document describes the schema available from the NavOnt namespace.
The NavOnt name spase is used to define the different properties used by NAV that could not be found in exsisting namespaces

Schema overview:
  employer:
      Definition: Information about the employer
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/blob/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#employer
  extent:
      Definition: The extent of the work. if it's a parttime(deltid) or fulltime(heltid) job
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#extent
  occupationCategories:
      Definition: Shows the level1 and level2 occupational categories for a job
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#occupationCategories
  positioncount:
      Definition: Positions availible for a job
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#positioncount
  relatedCourse:
      Defition: Show related courses to a job
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#relatedCourse
  sector:
      Definition: Shows the sector of a job and if it's for a public(offentlig) or private(privat) company
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#sector
  source:
      Definition: Shows the source of the job ad
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#source
  sourceurl:
      Definition: A url redirecting to the source of a job ad
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#sourceurl
  uuid:
      Definition: Nav unique identifier for job ads
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#uuid
  workLocations:
      Definition: Information about the work location
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#workLocations
  level1:
      Definition: one of the ocupational categoriy tags used by nav's search filtering
       URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#level1
  level2:
      Definition: the secon occupational cateogry tag used by nav's search filtering
      URI:https://github.com/M-Hatlem/info216/edit/master/Ontology/NavOntologyDefinition.txt#level2
